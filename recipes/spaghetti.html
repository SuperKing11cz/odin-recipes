Million dollar spaghetti recipe

Spaghetti on a plate

This is a recipe for lasagna. We will be making very good lasagna based on this recipe

This is a second paragraph to the recipe of million dollar spaghetti. I am sure it will be delicious.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Butter
  • Cottage cheese, cream cheese, sour cream
  • More cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti.
  2. Cook the beef on a stove.
  3. Prepare the baking dish the spread half of the noodles at the bottom.
  4. Top with the mixture of cheese, cream cheese and sour cream.
  5. Cover with remaining spaghetti and butter.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes, top with cheese, continue baking untill the cheese is melted.
