Lasagna recipe

Lasagna on a plate

This is a recipe for lasagna. We will be making very good lasagna based on this recipe

This is a second paragraph to the recipe of lasagna. I am sure it will be delicious.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato product
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
